Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing is the use of social media platforms and websites to promote a product or service. It involves creating and sharing content on social media networks to achieve marketing and branding goals.

TikTok/Reels

TikTok and Reels are short-form video platforms that allow users to create and share engaging content. They are popular for their creative features and viral potential, making them effective for reaching a wide audience.

Web Development

Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites. It involves web design, web content development, client-side/server-side scripting, and network security configuration.
